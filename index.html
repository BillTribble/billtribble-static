DJ sets, remixes and original tracks

Lucid Crystal Temple

Inspired by a spiritual experience in early 2020, these tracks are ambient reflections of a temporarily lucid state of mind. The entire EP was recorded using just one virtual modelling instrument over one week, using a microtuning designed to produce very clear, transparent sounds.

Spirit Guides

A cinematic, ambient electronic album featuring my vocals and field recordings from travels round Japan and Europe – created in a hypnotic, meditative state in late nights over two decades.

Le Garage Hermétique

A tribute album to visionary French artist Moebius (aka Jean Giraud). Stream the whole album here.
