1 Million Movers

I'm on a mission to get a million people moving.

💀 People are immobile everywhere. We get boxed into offices, cars, and sofas - glued to phones, TV's, and computers. When we do move, we're often locked into rigid routines.

💃 If you've experienced the power of Ecstatic Dance, you'll know how it can change everything. How it can help shift emotions - open up your body AND your heart. You'll have seen how it can transform your body and your relationship to it. How it can build community, and even inspire spiritual growth.

💪 I want to empower others to DJ, facilitate, and run their own dances - everywhere. Wherever there are people, we need conscious dance.

🌎 I can't do it alone. I need YOU to join our community, learn the skills, play at dances, put on your own events, and teach others. Together we can change the world.
